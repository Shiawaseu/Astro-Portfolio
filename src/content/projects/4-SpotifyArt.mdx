## SpotifyArt
SpotifyArt is a user-friendly web application that allows users to easily download high-quality covers for their favorite Spotify songs and albums. Whether you're looking to enhance your playlists or just want a beautiful cover art for your collection, SpotifyArt has you covered!

## Features
- **Full-Quality Downloads**: Retrieve stunning album and song covers in full resolution.
- **Simple Interface**: Navigate seamlessly with a clean and intuitive design.
- **iOS Shortcuts**: Enhance your experience with dedicated iOS shortcuts for quick access.

## Usage
- Visit the [website](https://spotify-art.vercel.app) to start downloading.
- Check out the [iOS Shortcut](https://github.com/Shiawaseu/SpotifyArt/releases/tag/Release) for usage even on your mobile phone.
